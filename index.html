BagKart Collection: Tradition Woven into Timeless Sustainability

Introducing the BagKart Collection — where heritage craftsmanship meets modern design. Rooted in traditional koodai (basket) weaving, this collection reimagines the iconic Chevron pattern using eco-conscious materials. Each handwoven piece tells a story of resilience and harmony, blending cultural roots with modern details — from muted tones to minimal metallics. No two are alike, celebrating human touch and slow luxury. By supporting local artisans with fair wages and training, BagKart becomes more than a product — it's a movement toward ethical, lasting beauty. Choose craftsmanship. Choose sustainability.
